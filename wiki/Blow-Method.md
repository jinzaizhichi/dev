The Blow Method
===

Here, I will discuss what I call the "Blow Method", which
is a method I attribute to Jonathan Blow.

*The Blow Method*:

> Take a core mechanic and explore the implications to their fullest

Jonathan Blow is a indie game developer and programming language designer.
His games include Braid, The Witness and he is the creator of the Jai
programming language.

The major points are discused in his IndieCade 2011 talk.
